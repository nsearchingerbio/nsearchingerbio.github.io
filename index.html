After attending middle school and junior high at the K-12 Potomac School in McLean Virginia, I attended St. John’s College High School (SJC) in Washington DC. At SJC I joined the crew team in which I participated for all three seasons for all four years. While at SJC I also joined the Army JROTC Regiment during my Sophomore and Junior years achieving the rank of Staff Sergeant. During my senior summer, instead of working at GW I decided to work at a CulinAerie a cooking school in Washington DC. After four years of high school, I decided to attend Fordham University in the Bronx. While at Fordham I am currently studying for a B.S in Computer Science.
